How to Work the Dashboard



Use the drop down bar to select the year you would like to see on the map then click Apply Changes. To see the WRI for a country, click on that country on the map. Use the Year and Country drop down to find all the specific values for the country and year you are curious about. Click Apply Changes after you have made your selection.


Once you have clicked on a country in the dropdown this will update the line graph below. The graph will show you the WRI, Exposure, Vulnerability and Susceptibility over the years for the country you have selected. Hover your mouse over the line graph to see the values for each year.

The dounut chart will show you information for the year and category you selected in the drop down bars after you have clicked Apply Changes. The dounut chart gives the number of countries that have Very Low, Low, Medium, High, Very High, and NA rankings for the category and year you are curious about.


The bar chart shows the Top 100 Contries with the lowest World Risk Index for the year you selected. Hover your mouse over the graph to see the specific value and the country.
